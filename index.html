Our Story

Mid Journey was born from a simple idea: commuters deserve more than just any food — they deserve great food. Located right at Ballabhgarh Metro Station, we're the perfect pit-stop for travellers, professionals, and students alike.

Founded by Vikas Jha, Mid Journey started as a passion project to bring big-city quality food to the metro commuters of Haryana. From Day One, the commitment has been unwavering: use only the freshest ingredients, never cut corners, and serve every customer with warmth and speed.

Today, Mid Journey is the go-to spot for burgers, subs, wraps, and beverages at Ballabhgarh — loved by thousands of daily commuters who keep coming back.
